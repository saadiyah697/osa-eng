About Us

Precise Engineering

OSA Engineering Co. Pvt. Ltd. is distinguished for its exceptional fabrication services and end products, made possible by the use of premium raw materials and advanced equipment. The company continually upgrades its machinery and technology to stay aligned with the latest industry innovations. Every product undergoes stringent quality control checks to ensure optimal reliability and minimal risk of malfunction or defect. Additionally, comprehensive post-sales support enhances the overall client experience. Timely delivery and constant communication throughout the transaction process reflect the company's strong commitment to customer satisfaction.

To maintain its high-quality standards, OSA Engineering has built a team of technically skilled professionals, combining seasoned expertise with a fresh perspective. Departments such as administration, production, quality control, fabrication, painting and finishing, sales and marketing, and packaging and logistics operate in close coordination, ensuring seamless operations. This commitment to excellence has earned the company a strong reputation and widespread recognition across the countries in which it operates.

WHO ARE WE?

OSA Engineering was established in 1998 in the UAE by Indian entrepreneurs to meet local market needs. Over time, our dedication to quality has expanded our reach to the Middle East and African markets. At the start of this decade, we launched operations in India, headquartered in Chennai.

We specialize in manufacturing and marketing Water Treatment Equipment, RO Plants, Wastewater Treatment Plants, Storage Tanks, Pressure Vessels, Industrial Filters, and Conveyors. We also offer highly reliable Pipeline Works and Fabrication Services.

Industries

Serving Diverse Industrial Sectors

Our growth and success have been based on our ability to foster strong and enduring relationships with our clients and partners. Our constant endeavor has been to provide viable, professional and cost-effective services globally in multiple strategic markets.

The key to nurturing lasting alliances is our ability to partner with each client to understand their industry and objectives. This allows us to deliver superior quality and value on every project, demonstrating our commitment to our customers.

Oil & Gas

Oil & Gas is the lodestone of all business activities in the Middle East. Be it upstream, downstream, offshore or onshore, every sector of the Oil & Gas industry has been a focal point for us.

Power & Desalination

We have served the Power Generation industry for many of their iconic projects in the region through both supply of vital components like pressure vessels and process skids, and also through construction engagements.

Water & Effluent Treatment

We partner with the leading contractors in water treatment in the region for supply of critical components that go into water treatment, waste water treatment and desalination plants, including Bulk Storage Tanks, Chemical Dosing Systems etc.

Food & Beverage

With a wide range of products specialized for this sector, including Heating Kettles, Filter Tanks, Cylindrical-conical brewery tanks, Agitators, Boilers, CIP systems, etc.

Construction

The construction industry has benefited from our state-of-theart equipment for their batching plants, material handling systems, and all the way up to steel structures for buildings and complexes.

Petrochemicals

Our scope for the Chemical industry encompasses complete concept to commissioning, right from FEED engineering to construction and handover. we have considerable experience of working with leading process technology Consultants and end-users.

Renewable Energy

OSA is the pioneer in the manufacture of wind towers for the renewable energy sector of the Middle East and North African marketspace.

Transportation

We are also the specialists in technology-intensive mobile process equipment, including trailer-mounted metering skids, batch mixers, mobile biocide injection packages, frac tanks, mobile fuel-dispensing units, etc.

Automotive

Supporting automotive manufacturing and service industries with high-quality steel fabrication, storage tanks, and component engineering.
